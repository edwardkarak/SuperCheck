What is the difference between "New" and "New Transaction"?

The "New" button creates a new account file. "New Transaction" creates a new transaction, which can behind either a deposit or withdrawal. The transaction is appended to the table. See New Account and Adding Transactions for more information.

Why does it say my starting balance is $0, even though I entered some transactions?

To create a starting balance, go to Edit->Change starting balance. Now the "starting balance" box in the lower right says the starting balance you entered. To change the starting balance, go to Edit->Change starting balance again, or double click on the first transaction.

Why do I not see the effects of changing the Settings?

This is because setting changes do not take place, until you restart the program.

How can I print my account?

To print an account, you can export it to an Excel file, and then print it out from within Excel.
