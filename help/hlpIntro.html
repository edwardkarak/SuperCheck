Welcome to SuperCheck!

SuperCheck is a powerful, convienent, and reliable application to keep track of the transactions you make. It resembles a real-life check register, with features to add transactions, edit them, and much, much more. In addition, you can change the settings to your liking, with preferences such as column widths, and default window size.
