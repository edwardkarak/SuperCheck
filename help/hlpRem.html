To remove a transaction, you must first click on the transaction you want to delete. Then, you can do one of the following:

  • Right click on that transaction, and click "Delete Transaction" from the context menu that appears
  • Press the DEL key on your keyboard
  • Press the Remove icon in the toolbar(Red "X" icon)
    • or
  • Press "Remove Transaction" in the "Edit" menu

(Note that all of the above things must be done while the transaction is selected; that is, it has a background color of blue).

When you do one of the above, a message box will appear, confirming if you want to remove the selected transaction. If you want to remove the transaction, press "Yes". If you have changed your mind, press "No".

Once you have deleted the transaction(s), it is recommended that you save your changes, either by pressing CTRL + S, the Save icon in the toolbar, or the "Save" in the "File" menu. (Unless you have made mistakes).
